About Me

I am a Merchandiser who just graduated from FIT with a BS degree in Fashion Business Management and a minor in Mathematics. I am passionate about creating data-driven solutions to improve decision-making processes in the fashion industry. During my college years, I was involved in several data analytics courses and I developed skills in different forecasting programs. I have also had internships in merchandising with Oscar de la Renta and Proenza Schouler and discovered that I really enjoy making use of statistical applications to retail merchandising practices.
